About

Hello visitor, you have safely landed on the personal page of Adrien Corenflos. If this is not what you expected, don't leave just yet, I don't have cookies, but I have a CV, some links to software projects, one or two blog posts about statistics, and even, if you are into that kind of kink, a bit of (French) poetry.

In case you are still here reading, here's a few facts about me. I am a PhD student under Simo Särkkä at Aalto University, in Finland. Winter is not that cold here, really. Prior to this I was working as a quantitative researcher for JP Morgan in London, and focused mostly on statistical modelling of corporate credit markets.
